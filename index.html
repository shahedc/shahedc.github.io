VRROOM

VRROOM is my Voluntary Resume Review and Optional Occasional Mentorship service, to help candidates who want to apply for techcnial jobs at Microsoft.

How to participate:

  1. Send me your resume (Word/PDF) or a link to your LinkedIn profile.
  2. Identify 1 or more jobs at Microsoft you would like to apply for, on MS Careers: http://careers.microsoft.com
  3. I'll set up a quick call to discuss your experience and provide tips to improve your resume for the jobs you're interested in.
  4. OPTIONAL: Once I am familiar with your work experience, I can refer you on the MS Careers site (if you'd like).
  5. OPTIONAL: Time permitting, feel free to reach out via email, LinkedIn or Twitter for additional guidance.
